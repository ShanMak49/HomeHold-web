FAQ

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings > Store > Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings > Export Database. This creates an encrypted backup file (.hcbackup) that you can share or save.

How do I import a backup?

Go to Settings > Import Database, then select your .hcbackup file.

How do I remove ads?

Purchase the "Remove Ads" item or VIP in the Store page.

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes. Link your Apple or Google account in Settings to restore purchases across devices on the same platform.
