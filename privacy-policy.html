HomeHold Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

HomeHold collects minimal data to provide its services:

  • Camera: Used solely to take photos of inventory items. Photos are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your Google Sheets if you enable sync.
  • Anonymous Account: A unique anonymous identifier is created via Supabase Auth to manage in-app purchases and sync data across devices.
  • Device ID: Used to associate purchases with your device. On iOS, a UUID is stored in Keychain. On Android, the system SSAID is used.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide inventory management features
  • To process and restore in-app purchases
  • To sync data across devices when you link an Apple or Google account
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Displays ads and may collect device identifiers for personalized advertising. You can remove ads via in-app purchase.
  • Supabase: Provides authentication and database services for purchase records and redemption codes.
  • RevenueCat: Manages in-app purchase subscriptions and entitlements.
  • Apple / Google: Processes in-app purchases through their respective stores.

4. Data Storage

Your inventory data is stored locally on your device. If you enable Google Sheets sync, data is also stored in your personal Google Sheet. We do not store your inventory data on our servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by the third-party services listed above to provide app functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

HomeHold is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You can delete all local data by uninstalling the app. To delete your account and server-side data, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
